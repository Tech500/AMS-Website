Getting Started

How It Works

01
Visit Open House

Drop in any Monday from 6–8pm. No appointment, no commitment. See the space, meet the community, try some equipment.

02
Fill Out the Form

Click Join Now and complete our Google Form registration. Choose the membership tier that fits your situation.

03
Sign the Waiver & Pay

Upload your signed waiver with the form or bring it to the shop. You'll receive an email to set up billing through Fabman.

04
Get Your Key

New members have a 30-day probationary period with access during events. After that you get your own key for 24/7 access.
