Who We Are

Mission & Values

The principles behind Anchorage Makerspace — why we exist, what we stand for, and the community we're building together.

Our Mission
To provide Anchorage with an open, inclusive workspace where anyone can learn, create, and collaborate — turning ideas into reality through access to tools, technology, and community.
Core Values
Value // 01
🔓
Open Access
Making it up doesn't require a degree or a budget. Our doors — and our tools — are open to everyone. Open House every Monday, no appointment needed.
Value // 02
🤝
Community First
We are member-run and community-driven. Decisions are made together, knowledge is shared freely, and everyone who shows up belongs here.
Value // 03
🧠
Lifelong Learning
Curiosity is the engine. We host workshops, mentorship, and hands-on training — because the best way to learn something is to build it.
Value // 04
🌍
Local Impact
Rooted in Anchorage. We support Alaskan makers, entrepreneurs, students, and artists — keeping creativity and innovation close to home.
Value // 05
🔧
Right to Repair
We believe in owning what you buy and fixing what breaks. The maker ethos is inseparable from sustainability and self-sufficiency.
Value // 06
⚡
Innovation + Safety
Pushing limits responsibly. We encourage ambitious projects while maintaining a safe, respectful shop environment for everyone who walks through the door.
